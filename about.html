ABOUT OUR CHAPTER

OUR INSTITUTION :

SSN College of Engineering (SSNCE) was established with the aim of providing quality higher education and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of education on a consistent basis. The campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere with combination of some of the finest graduate, undergraduate, and research programs, enhanced by the most skilful and experienced faculties, remarkable facilities, and a verdant landscape spanning a vast expanse of 230 acres amidst serene surroundings. Experienced and learned professionals strongly nurture the students. The global standards set at SSN in the field of teaching and research spur us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence. In fact, it has become a way of life for us. The highly motivated youngsters on our campus are a constant source of pride. Founded by the visionary Mr. Shiv Nadar, who serves as the illustrious Chairman Emeritus and guiding force behind HCL Technologies, notable to promote, conceptualize and bring about a paradigm shift through the development of outstanding leadership, research, knowledge and ideas for education and allied development sectors. Our renowned institution is named after the Chairman’s father, Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar.

Our Vision:

“To be a world class institution for technical education and scientific research for public good.”

Our Mission:

Upholding a steadfast commitment to merit-based admissions, the institution aims access to quality education through a robust scholarship program, ensuring that students receive world-class academic opportunities to realize their academic aspirations, to make a positive difference, to be a centre of excellence in education in emerging technologies , to focus on innovation, research, academic excellence and creativity to develop socially conscious leaders capable of addressing future challenges and to to build world class research capabilities on par with the finest in the world and broaden students’ horizons beyond classroom education.

Beyond the rigors of academics, the college life at SSN is enriched by a plethora of engaging extracurricular activities, including participation in sports, recreational pursuits, co-curricular endeavours, and cultural events. This holistic approach ensures that students not only excel in their chosen fields of study but also evolve into individuals with public spirit. Complementing the robust academic programs, the SSN campus is a testament to architectural excellence, featuring buildings with the state-of-the-art computer and internet facilities, modern workshops, seminar halls, auditoriums, and well-stocked libraries. The institution also boasts impressive sports and games fields, as well as an indoor stadium with a gymnasium, catering to the diverse interests and needs of the student community. With a faculty strength of 275 and a highly favourable faculty-student ratio of 1:15, the SSN College of Engineering stands as a shining example of academic excellence.

ISTE, IEEE and IETE are some of the technical organisations that are actively launching workshops, guest lectures, and conferences attended by students and researchers from all over India. NSS, YRC,NSO enkindle us in becoming hand-on hand with the society by conducting various activities.

Strengthening this legacy of excellence is a vibrant alumni network, with events every year serving as a platform for our alumni to reconnect, share their everlasting memories and experiences that made each find a path that felt right for them and contribute to the growth and development of their alma mater.

Truly, the SSN Institutions driven by a belief in the need for all-round development, is exactly the kind of place where one can discover one’s true potential, identify one’s passions, and accomplish one’s goals providing a unique and transformative educational experience, recognizing students to carve out distinctive paths for themselves and emerge as socially responsible citizens, fully equipped for their chosen professions.

IEEE

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, is the world's largest professional organization which aims to use advanced technology for asserting human needs. Established in 1963 through the merger of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers (AIEE) and the Institute of Radio Engineers (IRE). IEEE today has

  • Over 460,000 members in more than 190 countries, with more than 66 percent from outside the United States
  • More than 171,000 Student members
  • 344 Sections in ten geographic Regions worldwide
  • 2,709 Chapters that unite local members with similar technical interests
  • 3,635 Student Branches at colleges and universities in over 100 countries
  • 4,194 Student Branch Chapters of IEEE technical Societies

In addition to fostering member collaboration and knowledge exchange, IEEE sets industry standards for innovation and advances the development and application of technology to address societal concerns. IEEE is a major force behind technological advancement and the direction of engineering and technology around the world through conferences, publications, and educational activities.

Mission:

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Vision :

IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

Accomplishments in IEEE technical fields are recognized with annual awards for outstanding contributions to technology, society, and the engineering profession.

One of the hallmarks of IEEE is its dedicated focus on professional development and knowledge sharing. Through its extensive network of local chapters, technical societies, and affinity groups, the organization provides a platform for members to collaborate, exchange ideas, and stay abreast of the latest advancements in their respective fields.

IEEE's flagship publications, such as the IEEE Transactions and IEEE Spectrum magazine, are renowned for their high-quality, peer-reviewed content, keeping members and the broader scientific community informed about the latest breakthroughs and conferences.

Additionally, the organization hosts numerous conferences, workshops, and symposia around the world, fostering cross-pollination of ideas and facilitating the dissemination of knowledge.Beyond its academic and professional activities. Through its various humanitarian initiatives, the organization leverages the expertise of its members to address pressing global challenges, such as improving access to clean water, developing sustainable energy solutions, and enhancing the quality of life for underserved populations.

As a global leader in the advancement of technology, IEEE continues to inspire innovation, drive progress, and empower the next generation of engineers and technologists, ultimately contributing to a more connected, sustainable, and technologically-advanced world.

SIGNAL PROCESSING SOCIETY

This society is the world’s premier association for signal processing engineers and industry professionals. Engineers around the world look to the Society for information on the latest developments in the signal processing field. Its deeply rooted history spans 75 years, featuring a membership base of more than 20,000 deeply interested and involved signal processing engineers, academics, industry professionals and students who are all part of a dynamic global community – spanning 120 countries worldwide.

Mission:

The IEEE Signal Processing Society is an international organization whose purpose is to: advance and disseminate state-of-the-art scientific information and resources; educate the signal processing community; and provide a venue for people to interact and exchange ideas.

Vision:

The IEEE Signal Processing Society is a dynamic organization that is the preeminent source of signal processing information and resources to a global community. We do this by: being a one-stop source of signal processing resources; providing a variety of high-quality resources to a variety of users in formats customized to their interests; adapting to a rapidly changing technical community; and being intimately involved in the education of signal processing professionals at all levels.

The Society also oversees publication of numerous periodicals, including the IEEE Signal Processing Magazine and the Inside Signal Processing eNewsletter. Learn more about volunteer and employment opportunities, and how to Get Involved.

IEEE SPS – SSNCE:

The IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS) Student Branch was inaugurated in SSN College of Engineering on August 21, 2023.

The IEEE SPS Student Branch of SSNCE is a technical forum for giving international exposure to students apart from cultivating creative mind to do technical innovations by organizing various events like guest lectures, workshops, project expo, conferences and competitions on ongoing tools and technologies. It has also involved in hosting the IEEE SPS Seasonal School on "AI-based Medical System Design for Biomedical Signals "to equip participants with a comprehensive understanding of AI techniques applied to biomedical signals. From then, there has been no looking back we have been very active and vibrant till this date. Since the inauguration the number of student members joined in IEEE has increased. Since then, it has organized many events to provide outstanding value to its members. The objective of starting IEEE Student branch was to create awareness about the day today technological developments and to make them to utilize the resources available in IEEE. And to make most of our student members involve themselves in publishing research papers and projects.
